World Health Organization has estimated 12 million deaths occur worldwide, every year due to Heart diseases. Half the deaths in the United States and other developed countries are due to cardio vascular diseases.My attempt in this project is to do a prognosis of Chronic Heart disease by studying various behavariol, demographic and medical attributes in existing patients to find a pattern or trend that can help high risks patients to avoid falling victic to this disease by making certain lifestyle changes.
The dataset is from an ongoing cardiovascular study on residents of city Farmingham in Massecheuchets.It contains 4000 records and considers 14 attributes that are potential risk factors. I have done statistical analysis and vizualization using Excel and Tableu.
